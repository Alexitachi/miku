Miku is an open project: the software is open, the data are open, the documentation is open, and I welcome your contribution. Whether you fix a typo, add a book, or write a widget--it's all welcome. I need a small team of fantastic programmers because i can't do it alone!. One web page for every book ever published. It's a lofty but achievable goal. To build Miku, we need hundreds of millions of books, records, a wiki interface, and lots of people who are willing to contribute their time and effort to building the site.

How can you help? Improve the records we have for the books you love – every record is fully editable by clicking the “edit” button on the page. If we don't have a record for a book, you can create one. If you're a library with digitized resources, tell us where they are and we'll upload it for you. To get the source code click on my name it will take to you git repository.

In the forum in miku you can discuss topics in a book with the team: post questions, comment on it and document answers. If you watch a forum you will automatically watch all messages within this forum.To edit a message in a forum select the message from the forum and in the message details click the Edit button in the top right corner of the message. To create a new forum in your project, navigate to -> Book and click the Forum button.

If you did not find what you were looking for, If you need help from the community or want to support others, If you are eligible for professional support and have more questions - you can upload your query.

Miku will grow with time and will try to improve your experience.

♥
